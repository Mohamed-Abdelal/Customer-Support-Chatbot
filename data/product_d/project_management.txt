TROUBLESHOOTING:
Q: Notifications are not arriving?
A: Profile > Notification Preferences > verify channels. For email: check spam. For mobile: Profile > Mobile App > push notification permissions.

Q: How do I migrate tasks from Jira?
A: Settings > Import > choose Jira > download CSV from Jira > upload here. Map fields in the wizard. Migration keeps all comments, attachments, and history.

Q: Automations are not triggering?
A: 1) Check automation is enabled. 2) Verify trigger conditions match exactly. 3) Review Automation Log: Settings > Automations > Logs. Free plan has a 100 runs/month limit.

Q: How do I set up Single Sign-On?
A: Enterprise plan required. Admin > Security > SSO > choose provider (Okta, Azure AD, Google) > follow setup guide. Supports SAML 2.0 and OIDC.

Q: Can guests access projects without a paid seat?
A: Yes — invite as Guest (free). Guests can view and comment but cannot create tasks. Unlimited guest seats on all paid plans.
