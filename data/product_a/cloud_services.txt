TROUBLESHOOTING:
Q: Sync stops or files stuck?
A: 1) Open Settings > Advanced > Restart Sync. 2) Check that ports 443 and 8443 are not blocked by your firewall. 3) Clear cache: AppData/CloudStore/cache (Windows) or ~/Library/CloudStore/cache (Mac).

Q: Upload speeds are very slow?
A: Enable parallel multi-thread uploads in Settings > Performance. Ensure at least 10 Mbps upload bandwidth. Pause and resume large uploads if throttling occurs.

Q: How do I restore deleted files?
A: Web Console > Trash > select files > Restore. Files remain in Trash for 30 days before permanent deletion.

Q: How do I enable 2FA?
A: Account > Security > Two-Factor Authentication > choose TOTP app (Google Authenticator, Authy) or SMS.

Q: What are the API rate limits?
A: Starter: 1000 req/hr. Business: 10000 req/hr. Enterprise: unlimited (fair-use).

Q: How do I migrate from AWS S3?
A: Tools > Data Migration > Source: Amazon S3 > enter credentials > start wizard. Large migrations may take 24-72 hours.
