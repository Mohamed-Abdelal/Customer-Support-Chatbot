TROUBLESHOOTING:
Q: I forgot my master password — can you reset it?
A: No. Zero-knowledge architecture means we cannot recover your master password. Use your pre-configured Emergency Access contact or account recovery kit (printed at setup).

Q: Browser extension isn't auto-filling?
A: 1) Update the extension to latest version. 2) Go to Vault > Connected Apps > disconnect then reconnect. 3) Temporarily disable other password managers. 4) Check that the site is not safelisted from auto-fill.

Q: I received a breach alert — what do I do?
A: 1) Go to Security Dashboard > Breached Items. 2) Change the flagged password immediately. 3) Enable real-time breach alerts: Settings > Alerts > Breach Monitoring.

Q: How do I set up SSO?
A: Business plan required. Admin > SSO Configuration > choose provider > follow the SAML/OIDC wizard. Contact support@securevault.ai for enterprise onboarding.

Q: Can I share passwords securely with someone outside my team?
A: Yes — Items > Share > Create Secure Link > set expiry (1 hour to 30 days) > send link. Recipient does not need an account.
